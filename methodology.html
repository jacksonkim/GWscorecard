Frequently Asked Questions

How are grades converted to stars?

Letter grades (A–F) are mapped to a 5-star scale for accessibility and clarity. Half-star increments represent intermediate grades.

Why are some hospitals missing data?

Data availability may vary based on reporting requirements or incomplete disclosures. Missing values are treated neutrally and do not penalize hospitals.
